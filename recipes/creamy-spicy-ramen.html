Creamy Spicy Ramen

Description

Creamy Spicy Ramen is a simple dish for people who love spicy food but don't want the hustle. It tastes so spicy yet still flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag SamYang Buldak Spicy Chicken Ramen
  • 2-3 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 tbsp honey
  • 3 tbsp water/chicken soup

Steps

  1. Open SamYang Ramen package and boil the ramen without putting in any other materials inside.
  2. Once the ramen turns soft, scope it out and put other materials inside the package onto the ramen.
  3. Put the peanut butter and honey onto the ramen.
  4. Put the water or chicken soup onto the ramen.
  5. Stir until all the sauce is evenly distributed. Add more water or chicken soup if necessary, and enjoy the ramen!
