Pot Roast

Description

Pot roast is an American dish of meat, usually brisket of beef or chuck roast, stewed in one piece in a covered pot and served in its own gravy.

Ingredients

  • 3.3kg beef roast
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 3 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp parsley flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to one's taste
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp green seasoning/sofrito (optional)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2-3 cups beef broth (preferably one with red wine)
  • 1 pack pot roast seasoning
  • 1 tbsp/1cub beef bouillon
  • 1 fresh stalk of thyme
  • 1 fresh stalk of rosemary
  • 5 yellow potatoes
  • 3 carrots

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400F.
  2. Pat dry your beef roast and drizzle olive oil all over. Combine all spices and seasoning together (paprika, garlic powder, parsley flakes, Montreal steak seasoning, ranch seasoning, salt and black pepper). Sprinkle half of the mixture on the meat keeping the rest for later.
  3. In a Dutch oven on med-high heat sear each side of the roast with a tbsp of oil. Add the onions and garlic to the pot and saut√© for 5 minutes.
  4. Then add tomato paste and green seasoning and cook for 2 minutes. Deglaze the pot with beef broth and add the rest of the spice blend and the pot roast seasoning packet. Cook the gravy for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the beef roast, thyme and rosemary to the pot. Cover and place the pot in the oven. Dice your potatoes and carrots in quarters.
  6. Bake for 1 hour before basting the meat and adding the potatoes and carrots. Bake for another 1 1/2 hour. Serve with mash potatoes or any choice of side and enjoy!
